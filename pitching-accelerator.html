This is not a lesson package. The Academy is a 6-month coaching structure built around weekly instruction, independent lab training, and required at-home work. Your athlete knows exactly what to do each week — and we measure progress with consistent benchmarks.

Why Small Cohorts

Your athlete trains alongside the same group for the full six months. That consistency builds trust, accountability, and real standards.

Group work isn't about saving time — it's about learning faster. Pitchers see corrections in real time, absorb feedback from coaches and peers, and retain more because they're part of the process, not isolated from it.

They push each other through hard weeks. They celebrate progress together when goals are hit.

Development is hard. It's easier — and more effective — when you don't do it alone.
