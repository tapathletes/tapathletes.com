Key Changes

  • CTA shift: "Enroll" + "Request Info" — Two paths. Ready parent goes straight to schedule assessment (that's the enrollment process). Not-ready parent gets info to take home. No begging. No "book your free assessment" energy.
  • New section: "The Complete Pitcher" — Goes right after the hero. This is the thing nobody else does. Mechanics, command, velocity, game knowledge, mound presence, positional responsibilities. The page tells you WHAT before it tells you HOW.
  • Assessment loses its own section. The assessment is the enrollment process, not a selling point. It lives inside the enrollment section or as a brief note in "How It Works" — not as a standalone pitch.
  • Enrollment absorbs Prep. One section: here are your options (All-Facility, Hybrid, Prep). No need for Prep to be its own section.
  • Tone shift: informing, not persuading. The page reads like a program guide. Academy energy. "This is what we do. When you're ready, here's the door."
  • Page flow is now: What → How → When → Questions → Go. What your pitcher becomes → how the program builds that → enrollment options and pace → common questions → enroll or request info.
