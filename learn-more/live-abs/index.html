Why Live At-Bats
Bullpens build pitches.
Live at-bats build pitchers.

A clean bullpen and a competitive at-bat are two different sports. Hitters change the strike zone. They change tempo. They change what your stuff does in your hand.

Live ABs put your arsenal in front of a real lineup with full ball-flight data on every pitch. You see what plays and what doesn't — not on a leaderboard, on a hitter. The numbers tell you why.

Every session is run by a TAP Athletes coach. You leave with a pitch-by-pitch read on what's competing, what needs work, and the next thing to train.

How It Works
A one-hour session, built around competition
Every Live AB session blends a real opponent, professional-grade tracking, and coach-led adjustments. Show up, compete, leave with data and a plan.
Step 1 · Live Hitters
Compete in the box
You face hitters in real at-bats — full counts, sequencing, situations. Stuff that grades out in a bullpen has to actually get outs here. This is where pitchers find out what plays.
Step 2 · Rapsodo Tracking
Every pitch, measured
Velo, spin rate, spin axis, induced vertical break, horizontal movement, release height, and location — captured on every single pitch. The data is yours to take home.
Step 3 · Coach Feedback
Adjustments in real time
A TAP Athletes coach runs the session — calling pitches, reading the data, and making in-game adjustments. You leave knowing what to train next, not just what happened.
