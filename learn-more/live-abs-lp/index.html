Common Questions

Quick Answers

What ages and skill levels is this for?
Live At-Bats are open to pitchers ranging from middle school through college and beyond. Sessions are staff-supervised and structured around the pitcher's experience level — bring your stuff, we'll bring the hitters and the data.
What's included in a session?
A full hour of real at-bats against live hitters and every pitch tracked on Rapsodo (velo, spin, movement, location, release). A TAP Athletes staff member handles the Rapsodo setup so you can focus on competing — and you take your pitch data home. Coaching is optional and available as an add-on if you want active feedback during the session.
How do the multi-session packs work?
The 5-pack ($200) is good for 3 months and saves $25 vs. single sessions. The 10-pack ($320) is good for 6 months and saves $130 — the best value if you plan to come consistently. Sessions are scheduled as you need them.
Where is this and how do I book?
Indoor facility in Rogers, Arkansas (1751 S 1st Street). To book, click any "Book" button on this page or call/text (833) 860-2711. We'll get you on the schedule and confirm the session details.
Do I need my own gear?
Bring your glove, cleats, and pitching gear. We provide the hitters, the L-screen, the Rapsodo unit, and a staff member to run it. Showing up ready to compete is on you.
Can I add coaching to my session?
Yes. Coaching is an optional add-on for athletes who want active feedback during the session — pitch calling, in-game adjustments, and a debrief. Most kids who book Live ABs just want to compete and take their data home, which is why coaching isn't included by default. Mention it when you book and we'll quote the add-on.
